Image Translation Demo

The is a image translation project demo webapp which translates segmentation maps into photorealistic images using GAN neural networks.

Additional Info »

Try Example Seg Maps

You can experience the translation magic with the providing segmentation maps of the testing dataset, the sampled segmentation maps will on your left, and the resulting images will appear on your right, you can also download the images or compare two different models (pix2pixHD and SPADE).

Try the Demo »

Try Drawing Your Own

Apart from the example segmentation maps, you can draw your own segmentation maps with the simple drawing tools to see how well the model is doing, however, due to limited computing resouces, it may not perform well on the seg maps which are very different from those from the training dataset.

Try the Demo »
